About Me

I'm a Brisbane-based videographer with over six years of experience behind the camera. My journey began in 2019, documenting renovations with a Panasonic GH4—a camera with no stabilisation or autofocus that taught me to master the fundamentals the hard way.

Since then, I've evolved through countless hours of learning, experimenting, and refining my craft. From aerial cinematography with the Mavic 2 Pro to professional productions with my Blackmagic Pocket Cinema Camera 4K, I've developed a versatile skill set that spans testimonial videos and highlight reels.

As Media Prefect at Kedron State High School, I've led video productions including enrollment campaigns and school events. I'm proficient in DaVinci Resolve for editing and colour grading, bringing a cinematic quality to every project I undertake.
